The first enclosed version (200K constant bridge, 2Meg each from ADCs to Gnd, LM4040 2.5V ref) presents a constant 0.2239V on the DMM display.

Revised to the LM4060 (1.25V), there's a constant 0.086V on the DMM display

No pulldown resistor from A0/A2 to gnd with LM4040 (2.5V): 0.027V on DMM. Rises back to ~0.3V with pulldowns put back on. Confirmed proper function USB or battery powered.

Placing the PCB into diagnostic mode ups the ghost voltage to ~100mV. Not sure why. 

Swapping the LM4060 1.25V onto the breadboard version drops the ghost voltage down to 10mV.

Bumping the output impedance on the board to ~11MOhm (pair of 4.7M in front of the pair of 0.5M) drops the ghost voltage on the meter to 5.4mV, but kills the signal to barely visible.

Adjusting the bridge so that I have the 11M input with a 1M as the permanent part, and 200K as the switching part also kills the signal. Ghost voltage 12.8mV.
